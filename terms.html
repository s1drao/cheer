CheerClub is a service that lets friends record short voice messages ("cheers") for runners, which play in the runner's headphones during a run. By using CheerClub you agree to these terms.

Using CheerClub

  • Only record cheers you have the right to share; don't record anything unlawful, hateful, or harassing.
  • Cheers you record are delivered to the runner whose link you used and may be visible (by name) to others with the same link.
  • We may remove content or restrict access to keep the service safe.

SMS messaging program

CheerClub sends account notification text messages to users who opt in on our website: a notification when the cheer you recorded plays during a run, and an invitation when a runner you previously cheered for schedules a new run. Consent is given by entering your phone number in the optional opt-in form shown after recording a cheer. Consent is not a condition of using CheerClub.
