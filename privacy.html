5. SMS/Text Messaging Program & Consent ("CheerClub Alerts")

CheerClub operates an optional recurring SMS notification program for Cheerers. This Section, together with the SMS provisions of our Terms of Service, constitutes the program's disclosures.

5.1 What You Receive

If you opt in, we will send you: (a) a notification text when the Cheer you recorded plays in the Runner's headphones during their run; and (b) invitation texts when a Runner you previously cheered for schedules a new run and chooses to notify their cheer squad. We may also send you messages strictly necessary to administer the program (such as an opt-out confirmation).

5.2 How Consent Is Collected

Consent is collected exclusively through a web form on the Site: after you record a Cheer, an optional form asks whether you would like a text when your cheer plays. You opt in by typing your mobile phone number into that form and pressing the confirmation button. The form discloses that message and data rates may apply, that message frequency varies, and that you may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help, and links to this Policy and to the Terms of Service. Consent is not a condition of using the Service, of recording Cheers, or of any purchase.

5.3 Program Details

  • Message frequency varies based on your friends' running activity. You will typically receive one message when a Cheer you recorded plays, and occasional invitations when Runners you know schedule new runs.
  • Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier plan. CheerClub does not charge for the messages themselves.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP (or STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT) to any CheerClub message. You will receive a single confirmation message, after which no further messages will be sent to that number unless you opt in again.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any CheerClub message, or contact support@cheerclub.app.
  • Supported carriers: major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • Number changes: if you change or deactivate your mobile number, please text STOP from the old number or notify us so we can remove it.

5.4 The Mobile Information Non-Sharing Commitment

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the categories of sharing described in Section 7 exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third party. Phone numbers collected through the SMS opt-in are used solely to deliver the notifications described in this Section, are stored server-side only, and are never displayed to other users of the Service — including the Runner you cheered for.
