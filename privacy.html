CheerClub Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

CheerClub ("we", "us") lets friends record voice cheers for runners that play in the runner's headphones mid-run. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • From runners: phone number (used to sign in), display name, and run details you create (distance, schedule, progress).
  • From cheerers: the name you enter with a cheer, the voice recording itself, and — only if you opt in — your mobile phone number.

How we use it

  • To deliver voice cheers to the runner you recorded them for.
  • To send you the text messages you asked for: a notification when your cheer plays during the run, and an invitation when a runner you cheered for schedules a new run.
  • To show runners who has cheered for them.

Text messaging & consent

Providing your phone number is optional and happens only through an explicit opt-in form after you record a cheer. Message frequency varies with your friends' running activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages, or HELP for help.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories of information sharing described in this policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use service providers solely to operate CheerClub (Supabase for data storage and Twilio for message delivery), and they process data only on our instructions.

Retention & deletion

Voice cheers and phone numbers are kept while the associated accounts are active. To have your information deleted, contact us at sidrao1388@gmail.com and we will remove it promptly.
